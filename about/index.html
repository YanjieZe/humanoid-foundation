About Humanoid Foundation

Humanoid Foundation is a research initiative dedicated to advancing fundamental science and technology in humanoid robotics, focusing on motion retargeting, teleoperation systems, and human-robot interaction. We develop open-source frameworks that enable seamless transfer of human demonstrations to diverse robotic platforms, bridging the gap between human expertise and robotic execution through innovative learning and control approaches.
